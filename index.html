Things I've built

Kagura

Web app · End-to-end encrypted notes

A private notes app that encrypts everything locally with AES-256-GCM before it ever reaches the server. No email, no identity check — just a username and password that never leaves your device. Auto-locks after a configurable period of inactivity.

Why: my notes hold the most private, sensitive parts of my life — I wanted a place I could actually trust with them.

SkyDays

Web app · Family activity planner

A planning tool for parents and caregivers that answers “where should I take my kid today?” — surfacing kid-friendly activities and destinations nearby.

Why: as a new mom, I kept struggling to find places nearby that would actually entertain both my daughter and me.

Mindful Screen Time

Chrome extension · Intentional browsing

A Chrome extension that adds a pause before distracting sites: a daily timeline, visit counts, time spent, and a 7-day chart so you can decide consciously instead of browsing on autopilot. Tracks active time only, stores everything locally — no cloud, no analytics.

Why: my time is limited and I get distracted easily — I needed something that would gently snap me back on track.
